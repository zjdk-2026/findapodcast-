When Tier 3 volume justifies
Org principle: Resist hiring widely. Build one pod (Research + Pitching + you), run it at 6–10 Tours with zero defects, document every step as a checklist + Loom video, then duplicate the pod under a lead VA. Growth is copy-paste, not reinvention. Filipino VAs: ~$3–15/hr, 60–80% below Western rates for comparable quality. Start part-time, convert best performers to full-time.

90-Day Roadmap

From today to a repeatable, sellable, fully-delivered business with proof and referrals in motion.

Phase 1 — Days 1–14
Foundation
  • Finalise all four launch documents (Business Plan, Fulfillment Playbook, Sales Engine, Client Agreements)
  • Stand up tool stack: database, sending domains + warmup, CRM base, funnel builder, e-sign
  • Hire and onboard Research VA + Pitching VA against SOPs
  • Build master pitch-tracker and client Activity Dashboard template
  • Lock service agreement and written guarantee terms
Phase 2 — Days 15–45
Prove It
  • Sell 2–3 founding-client Tours at modest discount in exchange for testimonials + case-study rights
  • Run full fulfillment end-to-end; time every step; fix SOPs where they break
  • Get first bookings on the board fast — momentum and screenshots fuel the funnel
  • Stand up VSL landing page; start collecting leads
  • Add Account Manager VA as volume justifies
Phase 3 — Days 46–90
Systemise & Scale
  • Document every recurring task as checklist + Loom — nothing should live only in your head
  • Add Content Editor VA; deliver first Content Engine in full
  • Turn completed clients into case studies; switch messaging from claims to proof
  • Introduce second pod once first reliably runs 6+ Tours
  • MRR from Self-Managed covering fixed costs — every Tour sale is largely profit
What top-1% actually requires
Relevance over volume
Only pitch shows where the client's exact buyer is listening. Audience fit beats download count.
Voice & personalisation
Pitches that read like the client wrote them, referencing the specific show. Personalised pitches reply materially higher.
Follow-up discipline
Most replies come on touch 2 or later. A 4–5 touch sequence is where the bookings actually come from.
Outcome packaging
Turning each appearance into clips, content and leads so the ROI is visible, repeatable and shareable.
