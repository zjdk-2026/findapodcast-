# Landing Page Redesign — CEO-Level Plan

## Goal
Redesign the 3-tier offer section + roughly double landing page content with trust-building stats, social media FOMO, and podcast industry authority data — all sourced/backed.

---

## Problem Diagnosis

### Current Issues
1. **Iconic Partnership copy is too long for a card** — narrative Hormozi-style copy needs full-width real estate
2. **All 3 offers presented identically** — they're fundamentally different products (software vs. DFY vs. full revenue machine with a closer)
3. **Zero industry trust data** — no stats, no social proof, no FOMO triggers
4. **Page density** — the "Pick your speed" section is a wall of cards with no visual hierarchy

### The Fix
Restructure the entire flow so trust/uniqueness/authority is established **before** offers are presented, then present each offer at its natural level of detail.

### Section 4: The Three Tiers (Redesigned)

#### Design Philosophy
**Self-Managed + Podcast Tour** are "buy software" decisions — they can live in comparison cards.
**Iconic Partnership** is a "partner with us" decision — it needs full-width narrative real estate.

#### Self-Managed Software Card
- White/dark card (current design works, compact)
- Price: $497/mo
- Feature list (concise, bullet-style)

#### Podcast Tour & Content Engine Card

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Page gets too long → load time suffers | Lazy-load stats section below fold; keep hero fast |
| Stats feel like "data vomit" | Group into 4 clean stat blocks, each with 3-4 numbers max, contextual headline |
| Iconic Partnership section overwhelms | Use visual engine blocks (4 icons) so reader scans before reading |
| Mobile responsiveness | Test every breakpoint; stat bars collapse to 2-col on mobile, 1-col on small screens |
| Stats go out of date | Use year tags in source citations; easy to update annually |
