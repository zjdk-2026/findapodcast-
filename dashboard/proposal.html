Common Questions

Answered before
you ask them.

Which option is right for me?
Self-Managed if you have 2 hours a week and want to stay in control of every outreach decision. Podcast Tour if you want your calendar filled without touching the process. Tour + Content if you also want 15–30 pieces of social content coming out of every recording.
How fast does it start?
Self-Managed: first shows queued within 24 hours of onboarding. Tours: kick-off call booked within 48 hours, first pitches sent within the week. Most clients see their first reply within days.
What podcasts will I be pitched to?
Matched to your niche, offer, and ideal listener during the onboarding call. We filter by audience fit, interview format, episode recency, and host responsiveness. We never pitch you to shows that don't fit — volume without alignment wastes everyone's time.
Can I cancel the Self-Managed plan?
Yes — cancel anytime, no questions, no lock-in. Your dashboard stays active until the end of the current billing month.
What if you don't hit 20 bookings in 90 days on the Tour?
We keep working at no extra charge until all 20 are on your calendar. The final payment only happens when booking 20 is confirmed. That's the deal — and it's in writing.
