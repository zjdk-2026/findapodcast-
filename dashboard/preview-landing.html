Honest answers

Questions people ask
before they book the call.

"Will the pitches sound like me?"

Yes. Every pitch is written from your profile, in your voice, for that specific host. You review before it sends. Edit, rewrite, or approve as-is. Nothing leaves your inbox without your say-so.

"What if hosts don't reply?"

Most don't on email one. That's why auto follow-ups run unlimited until they reply or opt out. Most bookings come from follow-up two or later. And new shows hit your dashboard daily, so momentum never stops.

"I don't have a big following. Will hosts book me?"

Most hosts care about your story and what you give their audience. Not your follower count. We match you to shows where your expertise, not your audience size, is the draw. Many clients booked their first show with zero social following.
